About Me

Hello, I am Bethany Dixon! I’m an UX/UI Designer, who loves to unite elements of research to style guides and usability to design. My main goal is to contribute my skills and knowledge to create and develop products to share with everyone. I specialize in designing digital products such as apps and websites.
